Theme Overhaul: 3 Approaches

How to structure the 4 new themes (2 warm + 2 cool)

A

Sunrise & Ember + Frost & Abyss

Warm pair: Light "Sunrise Cream" (warm cream bg, brown text, amber accents) + Dark "Ember Dusk" (deep cocoa bg, warm gold accents)

Cool pair: Light "Frost Slate" (cool blue-white bg, slate text, indigo accents) + Dark "Abyss Indigo" (deep navy-graphite, ice-blue accents)

Maximum contrast between warm/cool. Each pair feels like a different product personality.

B

Sunrise & Ember + Arctic & Obsidian

Warm pair: Same as A — cream/cocoa with amber/gold

Cool pair: Light "Arctic Mist" (neutral white with subtle blue, teal accents) + Dark "Obsidian Night" (pure dark graphite, violet-indigo accents)

Cool themes are more neutral/modern — less "icy" than A, closer to what devs expect.

C

Sunrise & Ember + Sage & Onyx

Warm pair: Same cream/cocoa foundation

Cool pair: Light "Sage Meadow" (soft green-gray bg, forest accents) + Dark "Onyx Emerald" (deep charcoal, muted emerald accents)

Unique — the "cool" pair uses nature-inspired greens instead of blues. Distinctive but unconventional.

All approaches share: neumorphic surfaces, JetBrains Mono code, theme-adaptive ripples, vapor background on dashboard
